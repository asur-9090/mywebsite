Motivational shayri

Talvaro ke shaye per itihash hammara banta hai ,
Jis orr javani chalti hai us orr jamana chalta hai.

Aap jise sab kuch khokar pane ki tammana rakhte hai,
Ek wakt aisa aayega jab vo aapko mukt me melega,
to bhi manjur na hoga.

Uasi jagah per jaha kayi raste milenge,
Palat kar aaye toh, Toh sabse pahele tujhe milenge,
Agar kabhi tere naam per jang ho gayi toh ... Hum aise buzdil bhi..
Pehli sakht me khade milenge.

Vakif hai sab toh..
Fashane dard ke gauo kya ..
Jahen galib toh khajana koi lauo kya..
Voh khahte chubhte mere lafj unhe bahut...
Chirr degi meri khamoshi chup ho jao kya .
